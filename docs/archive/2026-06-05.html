The fact that BTC is bleeding while VIX remains at 15.66 suggests the unwind is happening in pockets (crypto, Japan equities) but not yet reflected in broad US equity vol. For crypto positioning: if BOJ hikes June 16-17 and USDJPY falls sharply, expect further BTC downside as carry unwind accelerates and USD liquidity tightens. Conversely, if BOJ disappoints and USDJPY rallies, BTC may find a short-term bid—but that scenario also raises intervention risk, which would be a larger negative. Near-term, crypto is a hostage to the yen carry thesis.
